Mission to Guatemala - 2019-2020

In February of 2019, I left to Guatemala to serve a full time mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. I lived there for over a year before returning to the States with the rest of my fellow missionaries due to the COVID-19 outbreak.

BYU Sophmore Freshmen Semester - Fall, 2019

After graduating highschool, I was accepted to BYU where I anrolled in the Computer Science major. I completed Fall semester before leaving on a mission to Guatemala.

Graduation from Timpview - 2019

In May of 2019, I graduated from Timpview Highschool. I had some sort of ribbon that meant I'd earned something special but it was a Latin term and I forgot it.

BYU PRISM Research - 2017-2019

In highschool, I began my work with the BYU Prism Lab as an unpaid intern. After several months with them, I was hired on as a research assistant. While there, I developed an Android app that allowed users to interface with the algorithms for UAV data collection that the group did.

Utah Boys State - June 2017

Also during the summer of 2017, I graduated from Boys State. Boys State is a national program run by the Sons of the American Legion. It is designed to give students real-life experience with the law, civics, and the election system.

Debate Nationals - June, 2017

In June of 2017, I competed in the national Congressional Debate tournament. The tournament lasted a week and was held in Burmingham, Alabama.

Vex Robotics State Championship - June, 2016

In highschool, I participated actively in the Vex Robotics club as club president. In 2016, we made it to the state championships for the first time in school history. We took 2nd int he championship tournament that year.
